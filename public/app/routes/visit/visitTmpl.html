How to Find Us

Take the Green Line E train to the Museum Quarter stop, or the Orange Line train to the Ruggles stop.

In a car, take Forsythe Way to Huntington Avenue. The museum will be on your right. Continue past the museum and turn right onto Museum Road to reach our parking areas.
